Parabolic path
(a)
At the extreme position, the velocity of the bob becomes zero. If the string is cut at this
moment, then the bob will fall vertically on the ground.
(b)
At the mean position, the velocity of the bob is 1 m/s. The direction of this velocity is
tangential to the arc formed by the oscillating bob. If the bob is cut at the mean position, then
it will trace a projectile path having the horizontal component of velocity only. Hence, it will
follow a parabolic path.
