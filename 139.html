13.Two billiard balls each of mass 0.05kg moving in opposite directions with speed 6m/s
collide and rebound with the same speed. What is the impulse imparted to each ball
due to other.
Ans:
Initial momentum to the ball A = 0.05(6) = 0.3 kg m/s
As the speed is reversed on collision,
final momentum of ball A = 0.05(-6) = -0.3 kg m/s
Impulse imparted to ball A = change in momentum of ball A = final momentum – initial
momentum = -0.3 -0.3 = -0.6 kg m/s.
