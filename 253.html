1.A planet revolves around on massive star in a highly elliptical orbit is its angular
momentum constant over the entire orbit. Give reason?
Ans:
A planet revolves around the star under the effect of gravitational force since the force
is radial and does not contribute towards torque. Thus in the absence of an external torque
angular momentum of the planet remains constant.
