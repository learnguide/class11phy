12. Read each statement below carefully and state with reasons and examples, if it is
true or false;
A particle in one-dimensional motion
(a) with zero speed at an instant may have non-zero acceleration at that instant
(b) with zero speed may have non-zero velocity,
(c) with constant speed must have zero acceleration,
(d) with positive value of acceleration must be speeding up.
Ans. (a)
True
(b)
False
(c)
True
(d)
False
Explanation:
(a)
When an object is thrown vertically up in the air, its speed becomes zero at maximum
height. However, it has acceleration equal to the acceleration due to gravity (g) that acts in
the downward direction at that point.
