15.Explain why passengers are thrown forward form their seats when a speeding bus
stops suddenly.
Ans:
When the speeding bus stops suddenly, lower part of the body in contact with the seat
comes to rest but the upper part of the body of the passengers tends to maintain its uniform
motion. Hence the passengers are thrown forward.
